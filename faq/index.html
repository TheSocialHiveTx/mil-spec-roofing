Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Briefings on our operations, services, and standards. If you don't see your question here, contact HQ directly.

What roofing services does Mil-Spec Roofing provide ?
Mil-Spec Roofing provides residential and commercial roofing services across Southeast Texas, including roof inspections, repairs, full roof replacements, storm and hurricane damage repairs, and preventative maintenance.
Do you work on both residential and commercial properties?
Yes. Mil-Spec Roofing handles both residential and commercial roofing projects, from single-family homes to offices, warehouses, and multi-building properties.
What areas of Texas do you serve?
We proudly serve homeowners and businesses throughout Houston, Pasadena, Baytown, Deer Park, La Porte, League City, Pearland, Friendswood, Clear Lake, Seabrook, Kemah, Texas City, Galveston, Alvin, Angleton, Missouri City, Sugar Land, Katy, Cypress, Spring, The Woodlands, Conroe, and surrounding communities. If you are unsure whether your property is within our service area, contact us to confirm availability.
Do you offer free roof inspections?
Yes. We offer free roof inspections to identify damage, assess roof condition, and determine whether repairs or replacement are needed.
How do I know if my roof needs repair or replacement?
Minor, localized damage can often be repaired. Widespread damage, recurring leaks, storm impact, or an aging roof may require replacement. A professional inspection is the best way to determine the right solution.
Do you handle storm, wind, and hurricane damage?
Yes. We specialize in storm-related roof damage common in Southeast Texas, including wind, hail, and hurricane impacts. We recommend inspections after any major weather event.
Can Mil-Spec Roofing help with insurance claims?
Yes. We assist homeowners and property managers throughout the insurance process by documenting damage and working alongside adjusters to ensure repairs align with approved claims.
What roofing materials do you install?
We install durable, manufacturer-approved roofing materials selected for performance in Southeast Texas weather conditions. Material options are reviewed during the inspection process.
Do you install impact-resistant or hurricane-rated roofing systems?
Yes. We offer impact-resistant and high-wind-rated roofing systems designed to provide added protection in severe weather environments.
Is Mil-Spec Roofing licensed and insured?
Yes. Mil-Spec Roofing is fully licensed and insured, ensuring all work meets local codes, safety requirements, and professional standards.
Do you offer warranties on your roofing work?
Yes. We provide workmanship warranties along with applicable manufacturer warranties on roofing materials.
Do you offer military discounts?
Yes. We proudly offer military discounts for active-duty service members, veterans, and eligible military families as a way to give back to those who have served.
What does “Mil-Spec” mean in your roofing standards?
“Mil-Spec” reflects our commitment to disciplined processes, high-quality materials, precise installation, and attention to detail—modeled after military-grade standards of reliability and accountability.
What sets Mil-Spec Roofing apart from other roofing companies?
We combine military-inspired discipline, local Southeast Texas expertise, and a commitment to clear communication, quality workmanship, and dependable service on every project.
How do I schedule a roof inspection?
You can schedule a free roof inspection by contacting us directly through our website or by phone. Our team will coordinate a time that works for you.
