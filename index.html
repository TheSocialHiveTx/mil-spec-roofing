Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I need roof repair or replacement?

A professional inspection can determine whether damage is isolated or widespread. We explain options clearly so you can make an informed decision.

Do you perform storm damage inspections?

Yes. We provide thorough storm damage inspections and help document issues commonly required for insurance claims.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We carry full licensing and insurance coverage to protect both our team and your property during every project.

What areas do you serve?

We serve homeowners and businesses throughout the greater Houston area and Southeast Texas, including all major suburbs and surrounding communities.

How long does a typical roofing project take?

Most residential replacements complete in 1-3 days depending on size and weather conditions. We provide clear timelines during the estimate process.
