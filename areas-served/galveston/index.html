Galveston Roofing FAQs

How does salt air affect Galveston roofs?

Salt air corrodes standard materials quickly. We use marine-grade fasteners and corrosion-resistant components to ensure long-term durability in coastal conditions.

Do you offer storm damage roof inspection in Galveston?

Yes. We inspect for wind damage, hail impact, and water issues, providing photos and clear recommendations so you can make informed decisions.

What materials work best in Galveston hurricanes?

Impact-resistant materials and enhanced fastening systems help manage hurricane winds. We also focus on construction that meets coastal building codes.

Can most leaks be repaired without replacement?

Many leaks can be fixed when the roof is otherwise sound. We trace the source, repair the failure point, and verify drainage to prevent recurrence.

Do you help with insurance claims?

We provide documentation, measurements, and clear scopes to simplify the insurance process. Our goal is to reduce confusion and keep things moving.

How long does a typical replacement take?

Most homes complete in 1-2 days depending on size and weather. We confirm schedules in advance and update you if conditions change.
